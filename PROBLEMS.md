# Identified Problems – Iran & Persian-Speaking Users

This document lists recurring, real-world problems affecting users located in Iran
and Persian-speaking users globally when accessing modern AI platforms.

---

## P1 – App Distribution Unavailable (Critical)

**Description:**  
Users in Iran cannot download or update official AI apps via Google Play or Apple App Store.

**Observed Behavior:**  
- App listings hidden or marked unavailable  
- No official explanation shown to the user  

**Impact:**  
- Users search for unofficial APKs  
- Increased exposure to malware and phishing  

**Severity:** Critical  
**Layer:** Platform Distribution / Compliance

---

## P2 – Silent Network Failures (Critical)

**Description:**  
Access attempts result in infinite loading, timeouts, or generic “Something went wrong” errors.

**Observed Behavior:**  
- No clear distinction between legal block vs. network error  
- Users assume a technical fix exists  

**Impact:**  
- Users attempt unsafe workarounds  

**Severity:** Critical  
**Layer:** UX / Error Handling

---

## P3 – Ambiguous Regional Availability (High)

**Description:**  
Lack of clear documentation stating that services are unavailable in Iran.

**Observed Behavior:**  
- Unsupported regions omitted silently  

**Impact:**  
- Confusion and distrust  

**Severity:** High  
**Layer:** Documentation / UX

---

## P4 – Persian Language Output Quality (Medium–High)

**Description:**  
Persian (Farsi) output may be unnatural in professional contexts and inconsistent in register.

**Impact:**  
- Reduced usability for legal and professional use  

**Severity:** Medium–High  
**Layer:** Localization

---

## P5 – Broken RTL in Word/PDF Exports (High)

**Description:**  
RTL layout breaks in Word/PDF exports for Persian content.

**Impact:**  
- Manual repair required  
- Professional use blocked  

**Severity:** High  
**Layer:** Document Rendering / Export
